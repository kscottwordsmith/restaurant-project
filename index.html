Little Bit of Beef started as three guys that were tired of asking the same question whenever they walked into a bar: "Where's the burgers?" Too often, they'd walk into a supposedly fine establishment, only to be met with mediocre-at-best offerings. The three vowed to provide an experience worthy of the American people.

Naturally, man cannot live on sliders alone, and so the three were asked to branch out. Their signature sliders were given proper accompaniment, and now stand as equals. But there's one great request forever on their lips: a Little Bit of Beef!
